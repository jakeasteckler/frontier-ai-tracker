Methodology & Definitions

"Government Deal" = a formally announced MOU, contract, investment partnership, or technology deployment between a frontier AI lab and a national government entity. Commercial availability alone does NOT count. National AI strategies without frontier lab ties do NOT count.

"Military Deal" = a frontier AI lab's technology is explicitly deployed to, contracted by, or formally partnered with a nation's military, defense ministry, or intelligence services.

US/Western frontier AI labs: OpenAI, Anthropic, Google DeepMind, Microsoft AI, Mistral, xAI, Palantir, Amazon. SpaceX included for military satellite/Starlink connections.

Chinese frontier AI labs: Huawei (Pangu/Safe City AI), SenseTime, Alibaba (Qwen), Zhipu AI, Baidu (ERNIE), DeepSeek, ByteDance (Doubao), Tencent (Hunyuan), iFlytek. Huawei "Safe City" deployments count where they involve AI (facial recognition, video analytics, predictive policing), not basic telecom.

Inclusion criteria: Any country in the top 50 by military budget (SIPRI 2024) OR top 50 by active troop size, excluding the US and China. El Salvador included as a special case.

Military budget data: SIPRI 2024. Active troop data: IISS Military Balance & Global Firepower Index (2024\u20132025). Sources: official press releases, company announcements, defense journalism, CSIS, SIPRI (2024\u20132026).
